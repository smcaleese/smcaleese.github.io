Hi, my name is Stephen. I live in Dublin, Ireland.

From September 2023, I will be studying a master's degree in Computer Science at University College Dublin with a focus on artificial intelligence.

Previously I worked as a software engineer at Amazon and studied Computer Science at Dublin City University. For more information about my work history, see my LinkedIn profile.

I am interested in Effective Altruism, AI and AI safety. I spend a lot of time reading and thinking about AI because I think the creation of general human-level or super-human AI could be the most important event in this century. I also find the idea of longtermism compelling and also the idea that we are in the most important century.

In my free time I enjoy running, programming, writing and reading.

  • Reading: I mostly like reading non-fiction books about topics such as AI, rationality, history and science. My virtual bookshelf is here.
  • Writing: I've written some articles about AI on LessWrong.
  • Programming: I have created some side projects which can be found on my GitHub profile.
