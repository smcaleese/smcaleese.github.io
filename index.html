About Me

Hi, my name is Stephen.

I live in Dublin, Ireland. I'm currently working at Amazon Web Services (AWS) as a software engineer on the CloudWatch monitoring tool.

Previously I studied Computer Science in Dublin City University and graduated in May 2022. During my degree, I did a software engineering internship at Rapid7. For more information about my work history, see my LinkedIn profile.

I am interested in Effective Altruism, AI and AI safety. I spend a lot of time thinking about AI because I think the creation of general human-level AI could be the most important event in this century. I also find the idea of longtermism compelling and also the idea that we are in the most important century.

In my free time I enjoy running, programming, writing and reading.

  • Reading: I mostly like reading non-fiction books about topics such as AI, rationality, history and science. My virtual bookshelf is here.
  • Writing: I've written some articles on Medium and my blog. I also write posts about topics such as AI on the LessWrong forum.
  • Programming: I have created some side projects which can be found on my GitHub profile. My most recent project is a Twitter bot that tweets quotes about AI.
